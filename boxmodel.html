CARTOONS:

Doraemon: This Japanese series follows the adventures of a robotic cat named Doraemon, who travels back in time from the 22nd century to aid a schoolboy named Nobita Nobi.

Patlu: An Indian animated series focusing on the comedic adventures of two friends, Motu and Patlu, in the fictional town of Furfuri Nagar.

Tom and Jerry: A classic animated series featuring the never-ending rivalry between a cat (Tom) and a mouse (Jerry).

Oggy and the Cockroaches: A French animated series about a blue cat named Oggy and his constant battles with three pesky cockroaches.

Peppa Pig: A British animated series that follows the adventures of Peppa, a cheeky little piggy, and her family and friends.

Mr. Bean: The Animated Series: Based on the live-action series, this cartoon follows the eccentric and clumsy Mr. Bean and his daily misadventures.

Little Singham: An Indian animated series about a young, brave, and intelligent police officer who fights evil and saves the day.
